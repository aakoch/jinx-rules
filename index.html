Jinx

Jinx is a game that starts when 2 people say the same word or phrase at the same time. When that happens, the first player to say "Jinx!" is the winner and the other player must buy the winner a can of Coke (or some other easily-available flavor).

Rules

  1. Upon speaking the same word or phrase, the two contenstants yell "Jinx!". The first person to yell "Jinx!" is the winner
  2. The loser may not speak until they have purchased a Coke and given it to the other player. While the standard unit is a can of soda, other sizes may be used instead.

Exceptions

Unless agreed upon before the jinx, there is no "pausing" of the game.
